Welcome to Jigsaw

Jigsaw Enterprises,LLC is a multi-faceted company that serves a wide variety of customers.We have expertise in all aspects of earthmoving,land forming,site development, and design-build projects. Jigsaw has acompleted many projects for governmental agencies,commercial companies, and industrial businesses which have ranged in size,complexity,and duration. Our company offers in-house engineering services to provide the best overall value to our clients

EXPERTISE
Jigsaw provides a diverse array of land forming services and is experienced working within the construction management, design/bid/build and design/build project delivery formats. We have successfully performed publicly, privately and hybrid publicly/privately funded projects.

 Our portfolio of successfully completed projects spans decades and incorporates a diverse array of project types including transportation, energy, industrial, environmental, historical, sports and recreational facilities, and property development both commercial and residential.

 Our customers come from many sectors both public and private including federal, state, and local government agencies, public utilities, manufacturing, industrial, commercial, communication businesses, and energy both coal and gas.
OUR PORTFOLIO
Our portfolio of completed projects includes residential subdivisions,golf courses,sports fields,tourism sites,road and bridge contstruction,commercial site development,abandoned mine reclamation,cell tower site development,electrical and underground utility site development,storm drainage projects slope stabilization projects,sediment control projects,mass excavation,stream mitigation, and enclosed space excavation.
QUALITY PERSONNEL
We have an experienced,skilled team capable of working safely and productively within complex project requirements. Our cross-functional team members meet both OSHA(Occupational Safety and Health Administration) and MSHA(Mine Safety and Health Administration)safety training requirements. Each employee is equipped with the training,knowledge, and skills necessary to adapt to any jobsite requirements.

WHO WE ARE

Jigsaw Enterprises L.L.C. is land forming company with an engineering reach, focusing on sustainable development projects, operating in both prime and sub-contractor roles as project alignment dictates. Our diversified equipment fleet is staffed by a fully trained and experienced workforce guided by a management team with almost 200 years combined experience. Having successfully completed a wide variety of projects in a broad spectrum of fields in both mining and civil disciplines, we are prepared to meet the challenges of any earth moving or site development project.
